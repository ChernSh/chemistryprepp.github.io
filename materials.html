Courses

We have worked hard on this selection of courses. The options below include both paid and free courses of three levels. Lower level - the foundation of chemistry, the beginning of study. Secondary level - the middle, more advanced level. High level - the highest level, which is certified and provides higher or secondary technical education.

Such courses will be useful for people who find it difficult to learn the material on their own. If you are confident that you are capable of doing so, you can find a selection of self-study materials below.
